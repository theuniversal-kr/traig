The Liquidity Problem

Hired Liquidity vs. Organic Liquidity

Most exchanges pay market makers for liquidity. It works — until the contract ends.

Hired Market Makers

  • - Expensive monthly retainers
  • - Liquidity disappears when contracts end
  • - Misaligned incentives — they optimize for rebates, not your book
  • - Often the same firms across competing exchanges

traig-Powered Users

  • Zero cost to the exchange — users run their own strategies
  • Liquidity grows with user base — scales naturally
  • Aligned incentives — users want good fills, just like you
  • Persistent — strategies run 24/7 as long as users are active
