Philosophy

The principles that guide everything we build.

Your Keys. Your Funds. Always.

100% non-custodial architecture. Connect via exchange API keys. traig never touches your assets.

Your exchange, your credentials, your control. We simply execute the strategy you define.

Systems Over Speculation

We don't try to predict where the market is going. That's your call — or maybe you don't need to either.

Instead, we build systematic, rules-based strategies that perform consistently.

Define clear rules for every price level. No emotional decisions, no FOMO, no panic.

The market is uncertain. Your strategy doesn't have to be.

Nothing Hidden

Every AI prompt is open and auditable.

You can verify exactly what the AI does with your strategy.

Complete transparency in every operation — from order placement to strategy transitions.

We publish what we prompt. If you can't verify it, you shouldn't trust it.

Smooth Over Rigid

Markets are continuous. Strategies should be too.

Piecewise-linear recipes adapt naturally to price movements.

No rigid grid steps. No dead zones. No wasted ranges.

Every price level has a defined behavior — aggressive where you want it, conservative where you need it.
