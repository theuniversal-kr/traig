About traig

Systematic crypto trading, built from conviction.

What is traig

traig is an automated crypto trading platform built around a simple conviction: systematic beats speculative.

  • Continuous piecewise-linear strategies — not grid bots
  • AI-managed strategy creation and modification through natural conversation
  • 100% non-custodial — your exchange, your keys, your assets
  • Fully transparent — every AI prompt open and auditable
  • Built for both individual traders and institutions

Built in Korea, for the World

traig is built in South Korea. However, due to Korean regulations on crypto trading services, traig is not available in the Korean market.

We serve traders globally — and hope that one day, regulations will allow us to serve our home market too.

Global Vision

Our goal is simple: make systematic trading accessible to everyone, everywhere — regulations permitting.
