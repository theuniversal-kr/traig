How It Works

Three steps from idea to validated strategy.

1

Select a Recipe

Conservative by Design

  • We use conservative fill assumptions — orders fill only within OHLC range.
  • Maker-only execution model matches real trading conditions.
  • No optimistic assumptions. What you see is a realistic lower bound.
